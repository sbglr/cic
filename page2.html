Mission

Create a local small business community group that can be duplicated and expand its reach community-wide, state-wide, nation-wide, and world-wide.

Recruit committed membership of at least 25 members with diversity in age, geography, industry, thinkers, doers, personality, and ethnicity.
Identify business creators and innovators and entrepreneurs and small business-owners community-wide who want to learn how to be successful practitioners.

Be a creativity and innovation strategic resource for our entrepreneurial and business community and small business owners who have challenges and issues.

Create unique selling proposition to communicate who we are and what we do.

Create a creativity and innovation creed to a guide our beliefs, behavior, and actions.

Create creativity and innovation organization logo and motto.
Collect a database of creativity and innovation ideas, strategies, tactics, tools, techniques, tips, exercises, assessments, and quizzes.

Create an engaging experience that embraces, promotes, and applies creativity and innovation. Second, we will educate, inspire, and empower entrepreneurs and small business owners to apply creativity and innovation in their life, community, and business. Third, keep people coming back for more!

Create an online presence via web, social and new media by creating unique content to promote creativity and innovation and its benefits.
Create a creativity and innovation speakers bureau to communicate and reach out to the business and stake-holders community.

Highlight and promote local entrepreneurs and small business owners who are successful creativity and innovation practitioners.
